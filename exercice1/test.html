Médecin brillant  injustement accusé ou tueur en série au mobile énigmatique? Un anesthésiste-réanimateur poursuivi pour 30 empoisonnements de patients lors d'opérations, dont 12 mortels, s'apprête à être jugé en France lors d'un procès fleuve.

Fechier P, an anaesthetist now suspected of poisoning patients in 32 cases (with 13 lethal outcomes) at the Besancon hospital arrives at the Besancon courthouse on March 8, 2023. Is Frederic Pechier, the brilliant anaesthetist and intensive care doctor, an innocent man who has been wrongly accused, or one of the worst serial killers of the post-war period, responsible for 30 cases of aggravated poisoning of patients, 12 of which were fatal? The courts will have to decide at the end of a lengthy trial that begins on September 8, 2025 in Besançon. AFP
